About Us

A Home Filled With Warmth & Purpose

Founded in 2013 by Marisol Aguilar, RN, Sunrise Comfort Adult Care Home was built on a single conviction: every senior deserves to thrive, not just survive. Nestled in a quiet Bellevue neighborhood, our home provides a nurturing setting where residents are treated like family.

Our team of licensed caregivers and nursing professionals craft individualized care plans, ensuring that the unique story, preferences, and health needs of each resident are honored every single day.

What We Offer

Exceptional Care Tailored to Every Resident

From around-the-clock nursing support to joyful daily activities, our services are designed to nurture the whole person—body, mind, and spirit.

🏠

Private Rooms

Cozy, furnished private and semi-private rooms with cable TV, Wi-Fi, and phone connections. Residents personalize their space to feel truly at home.

💊

Medication Management

Our licensed nurses handle all medication schedules, coordination with physicians, and monitoring to ensure safety and proper dosing every day.

🍽️

Home-Cooked Meals

Three nutritious, home-cooked meals and snacks daily, tailored to dietary restrictions, cultural preferences, and physician recommendations.

🌿

Personal Hygiene & Grooming

Compassionate assistance with bathing, dressing, grooming, and incontinence care—always delivered with dignity and respect.

🎵

Enriching Activities

Daily activities including music therapy, light exercise, gardening, movie nights, birthday celebrations, and seasonal community events.

🩺

Home Doctor Visits

We coordinate visits from physicians, podiatrists, occupational therapists, physical therapists, and other on-call professionals directly to our home.

Contact Us

Sunrise Comfort offers a true home setting with a maximum of six residents, ensuring highly personalized attention from our certified nursing staff. Our owner, Marisol Aguilar RN, is available around the clock, and every care plan is created and overseen by our house Registered Nurse. Families consistently tell us the warmth, consistency, and genuine relationships formed here set us apart from larger facilities.

Our all-inclusive rate covers room and board, three home-cooked meals and snacks daily, 24-hour awake staff, medication management, personal hygiene assistance, housekeeping and laundry, activities, and coordination of on-call specialist visits. We believe in transparent, straightforward pricing with no surprise add-ons. Contact us for current rates.

Yes, we are licensed to accept Medicaid (DSHS-funded) residents, as well as private pay arrangements. We do not directly bill Medicare for room and board, but Medicare may cover eligible skilled nursing visits, therapy, and physician services provided in our home. Our staff are happy to help you navigate insurance and funding options.

Simply call us or fill out the contact form below to schedule a complimentary tour. During the visit you'll meet our staff, see the home, and we'll complete an informal needs assessment. If Sunrise Comfort is the right fit, we'll walk you through evaluation paperwork and coordinate a smooth transition with your loved one's current care team.

Family is always welcome at Sunrise Comfort. We encourage regular visits and family participation in activities and celebrations. We kindly ask that visits after 9 pm be arranged in advance out of consideration for sleeping residents, but there are no strict limitations. We also offer video call setup for families who live farther away.

With only six residents, availability is limited. We encourage you to reach out as soon as possible to check current openings and get on our waitlist if needed. Call us directly at (425) 784-1930 for the fastest response.
